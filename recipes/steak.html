steak

This is what a finished steak looks like

Steak is cooked med rare on a cast iron skillet.

It is butter basted and served hot oooo yeaa

  • Butter
  • Steak, Ribeye
  • Grapeseed oil
  • Steak Seasoning and Salt
  1. Get cast iron over medium-high heat for a good 15 minutes
  2. Add your oil and wait until shimmering
  3. Add your steak and let it sit for 3-4 minutes. Add your butter as well
  4. While cooking one side, take a spoon and baste butter onto the exposed side of steak repeatedly
  5. Flip steak, repeat basting
  6. Use thermoter to take steak off when its at 125 degrees F
